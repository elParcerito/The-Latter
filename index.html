The Latter – Game Rules

🎯 Objective

Be the team or player with the most points by answering questions correctly from different levels: Easy, Medium, and Hard.

🟢 Levels of Difficulty

  • Green (Easy) – 1 point
  • Yellow (Medium) – 3 points
  • Red (Hard) – 5 points

👥 Number of Players

Minimum 2 players or 2 teams.

▶️ How to Play

  1. One player selects a card (or question) from any level.
  2. The host or game master reads the question aloud.
  3. The player/team has 10 seconds to answer.
  4. If correct → they earn points based on difficulty.
  5. If incorrect → no points, and the turn passes to the next player/team.

⏱️ Time Limit

Each player/team has 10 seconds per question.

🧠 Special Rule

Each team may skip one question per game without penalty.

🏆 Winning

The first team to reach 30 points wins the game.
